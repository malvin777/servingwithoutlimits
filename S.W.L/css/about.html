Who are we?

This is what we do:

We are an organasation that aims to serve the communities in which we reside. Although injustice is all around us as Christians we should not look the other way, as an organasation we have chosen to take an active role in correcting the injustice through helping others. By teaching, preaching and praying, looking at Christ as our example and working toward the salvation of many.

We are an Youth run organasation that aims to be of service to the people in our communities in a practical way

By Empowering skills, giving to the needy and showing love and compasion to everyone in society

By leading from the front we aim to inspire Youth all over the world to seek a deeper purpose through community outreach and social responsibilty acts.
